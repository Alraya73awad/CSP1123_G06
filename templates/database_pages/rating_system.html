Rating System

What Your Rating Means

Rating represents your performance against other players. Win battles to climb, lose battles and your rating drops. Strong wins against tough opponents move you faster than stomping a lower-ranked rival.

Rank Tiers

Your rating places you into a tier. These tiers are more than titles, they hint at the kind of players and builds you'll face in ranked battles.
