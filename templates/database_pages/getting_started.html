Getting Started

Welcome to Clash of Code!

In Clash of Code, you create and train AI bots to battle against other players' bots. Your goal is to climb the leaderboard by winning battles and improving your bot's stats.

Step 1: Create Your First Bot

  1. Navigate to the Dashboard
  2. Click "Create Bot"
  3. Choose a name and algorithm
  4. Allocate your starting stat points

Step 2: Enter Your First Battle

Head to the Battle Arena to find opponents within your rating range. Your first battles will determine your initial ranking!
