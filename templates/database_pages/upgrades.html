Upgrades

Character Upgrades

Upgrades are consumable modules you can purchase on the Character page. They permanently enhance a single bot, so choose carefully based on the role you want that bot to fill.

Armor Plating

Reinforces your frame with dense alloy layers. Great for defensive builds and any bot that is expected to take sustained damage.

Overclock Unit

Pushes your clock speed beyond safe limits. The burst of speed is a big deal in turn order and evasion, but it is not free power. Pair it with a strong energy plan.

Regen Core

A slow but steady repair routine that keeps your bot online longer. Best on endurance builds that want to survive long engagements.

Critical Subroutine

A razor sharp targeting routine that increases your chance to land decisive hits. It pairs well with high Entropy and aggressive playstyles.

Energy Recycler

Reclaims wasted power during combat. This is a flexible upgrade that helps almost any build stay active for longer fights.

EMP Shield

Insulates your core from disruptive effects. If your bot relies on consistent ability usage, this can keep your strategy intact under pressure.

Upgrade Tip

Focus your upgrades on a clear role instead of spreading them thin. A bot built to do one thing well is easier to pilot and easier to improve.
