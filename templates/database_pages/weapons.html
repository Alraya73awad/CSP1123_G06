Weapons

Why Weapons Matter

Weapons define how your bot delivers damage. Each weapon adds offensive power and sets the combat style: close-range pressure or long-range precision.

Melee vs. Ranged

Melee weapons are built for direct impact and thrive in tight, aggressive fights. Ranged weapons trade raw pressure for flexibility and safer openings. Certain arenas subtly favor one style over the other.

Tiers and Progression

Weapons come in tiers, with higher tiers offering stronger base output. You can level weapons up over time, gradually improving their damage potential without changing your bot's core stats.

Equipping Gear

A bot can only equip one weapon at a time. If you equip a new weapon, the previous one is automatically unequipped. Consider your algorithm and stat build before locking in your loadout.
