Algorithms

What Algorithms Do

Algorithms are the AI cores that shape how your bot fights. Each algorithm applies fixed stat multipliers that are factored into combat and into the stat previews you see on the dashboard and manage screens. Pick one to define your bot's identity: raw damage, fortified defense, balanced stability, high speed, adaptive variance, or unpredictable chaos.

How Modifiers Are Applied

Algorithm effects are multiplicative buffs/nerfs on base stats. For example, PROC and DEF are scaled by the algorithm multiplier, then used in combat formulas. If an algorithm does not list a stat, that stat stays unchanged.

Weapon attack is added to base PROC, then the algorithm PROC multiplier is applied. This means aggressive algorithms amplify weapon damage more than defensive ones.

Algorithm Catalog

VEX-01 - Aggressive
Vexor Assault Kernel. +15% PROC, -10% DEF. Ideal for burst damage and fast takedowns, but fragile if fights drag on.

BASL-09 - Defensive
Bastion Logic Framework. +20% DEF, -10% CLK. A fortress AI that shrugs off hits but acts later and dodges less often.

EQUA-12 - Balanced
Equilibrium Core Matrix. No buffs or nerfs. Best for flexible builds or testing weapons without algorithm bias.

ADAPT-X - Adaptive
Adaptive Pattern Synthesizer. +5% ENT, -10% PROC. High-variance playstyle that leans into crits and rare effects at the cost of raw output.

RUSH-09 - Speed
Rapid Unit Synchronization Hub. +20% CLK, -10% DEF. Strikes first and dodges more, but can crumble under sustained pressure.

CHAOS-RND - Random
Chaotic Execution Driver. No fixed stat multipliers. The build relies on standard combat randomness (crits, dodges, arena variance) to swing outcomes.

XP Multipliers

Algorithms also affect how fast your bot levels in battle. Higher-risk cores earn more XP: VEX-01 (1.15x), BASL-09 (1.10x), EQUA-12 (1.00x), ADAPT-X (1.20x), RUSH-09 (1.10x), CHAOS-RND (1.30x).
