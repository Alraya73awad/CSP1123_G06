Arenas

The Battlefield Matters

Arenas are not just scenery. Each arena has its own atmosphere and tactical feel, nudging certain weapon styles or defensive setups to shine. Learning the arenas helps you pick the right bot for the job.

Ironclash Colosseum

A brutal pit where close combat thrives. Melee builds feel right at home here, while ranged kits can struggle to keep distance.

Skyline Expanse

Open skies and long sightlines give ranged weapons a clear advantage. If you rely on blades or fists, plan to close the gap fast.

Neutral Arena

No tricks, no bias. This is the proving ground where balanced builds and smart stat planning win the day.

Frozen Wastes

Treacherous footing and biting winds slow the pace of combat. Durable bots and steady damage tend to outlast flashy bursts here.
